# Bio

<div class="content-box" markdown="1">
Anurag Khandelwal is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Yale University, where his group focuses broadly on problems in computer systems, networks and security. He received his Ph.D. from UC Berkeley, where he was advised by Ion Stoica. His work has been recognized by an NSF CAREER award, two NetApp faculty fellowships, a Roberts Innovation Award, best paper awards at USENIX Security’20, ISCA’23, EuroSys’24, ISCA’25, ASPLOS'26 and IEEE Micro Top Picks selections in 2023 and 2025.
</div>
